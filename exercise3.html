Personal Profile

Sherlock Holmes

"I'm a consulting detective. The only one in the world. I invented the job."

William Sherlock Scott Holmes, known simply as Sherlock Holmes, is the world's only consulting detective, a profession he created for himself. He is based in London and often consulted by Greg Lestrade of New Scotland Yard, usually taking his best friend and former flatmate, John Watson, on cases.
